Aspirants Marching
Falcon

Aspirants-Marching Falcons are a 70 plus-piece Filipino traditional marching band from Daet, Camarines Norte.
The band formed in 2022 from a collection of Hard-hitter drummers and Graceful colorguards who met from different schools and who all just couldn't get enough DCI and WGI.
Their unique brand of entertainment fuses all genre melody and Instrumental harmonies with fantastic drumline and flashy choreoraphy that will make you wany to dance all night.
Once hailed as 'Champions of the old' In Camarines Norte, performed at the iconic Division and regional competition.
